What is a CV?

This is a document that shows your qualities when applying for any job. It includes your education, work experience and key skills to show that you are right for the role.

Start building your's now!

Follow the steps below and make your own CV to start applying for jobs.

Step 1: About You

Incude your name, full address and postcode and email address at the top of your document.

Step 2: Schooling

Talk about where you went to school or any training programs you completed. List the most recent education first. Include what you studied and any certificates or diplomas you received.

Step 3: Work Experience

List the jobs you've had, starting with the most recent one. For each job, write down the job title, where you worked, and what your main tasks were. If you helped achieve something great, mention that too!

Step 4: What You're Good At

Share the skills you have that make you great at your job. This could be languages you speak, computer programs you know, or other things you do well.
